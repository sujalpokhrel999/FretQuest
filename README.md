# FretQuest 🎸 — Free Online Guitar Tuner, Scale & Chord Trainer

**FretQuest** is a free, browser-based, gamified guitar practice app that helps you **learn guitar online** through real-time pitch detection — no downloads, no signup, no gear required beyond your guitar and a microphone.

🔗 **Live App:** [fretquest.lovable.app](https://fretquest.lovable.app)

Practice guitar like a game, not a chore. FretQuest listens to what you play in real time and scores you instantly — turning solo practice into an interactive skill-building loop.

---

## 🔍 What is FretQuest?

FretQuest is a **free online guitar tuner and guitar trainer** built for beginners and intermediate players who want to:

- Tune their guitar accurately using real-time pitch detection
- Learn guitar notes and scales through gamified drills
- Practice guitar chords with instant feedback on accuracy
- Build fretboard fluency with a pentatonic riff and lead trainer

Whether you're searching for a **guitar scale trainer**, a **free chord trainer online**, a **gamified guitar practice app**, or simply want to **learn guitar online for free**, FretQuest is built to be the fastest way to start playing better — directly in your browser.

## ✨ Features

| Tool | Description |
|---|---|
| 🎯 **Visual Tuner** | Real-time, mic-based guitar tuner with accurate pitch detection |
| 🎵 **Notes & Scales Trainer** | Prompted note and scale drills with streaks, points, and high scores |
| 🎸 **Chord Trainer** | Listens for full chord shapes and confirms when you play them correctly |
| 🔥 **Riffs & Leads Trainer** | Pentatonic riff challenges with an optional rhythm/timing mode |

No installs. No paywalls. No login walls. Just open the site and start playing.

## 🎯 Why FretQuest?

Most guitar tuner apps stop at tuning. Most guitar training apps are locked behind subscriptions (Yousician, GuitarTuna Pro, Fret Trainer). FretQuest is different:

- ✅ **100% free** — no subscription, no premium tier
- ✅ **Runs in-browser** — no app store, no install
- ✅ **Real-time audio feedback** — not just static lessons, actual listening and scoring
- ✅ **Gamified** — scores, streaks, and challenges instead of passive video lessons

Perfect for **self-taught guitarists**, **guitar teachers** looking for a free student practice tool, and **hobbyist musicians** who want a fun way to build fretboard skills.

## 📖 How to Use FretQuest

1. Visit [fretquest.lovable.app](https://fretquest.lovable.app)
2. Allow microphone access when prompted
3. Choose a mode: **Tuner**, **Notes & Scales**, **Chord Trainer**, or **Riffs & Leads**
4. Play your guitar and get instant, real-time feedback
5. Track your streaks and scores as you improve
